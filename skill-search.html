Project information

  • Category: Social Media design
  • Client: Skilled Search
  • Project date: 01 March, 2024
  • Project URL: www.skilledsearch.co.uk

Social Media design for Instagram & Facebook

Design Thinking:
  • Consistency:The brand maintains a consistent layout and messaging across all images, reinforcing its identity.
  • Clarity:The focus is on clear communication of services, values, and processes, making it easy for the audience to understand the offerings.
  • Call to Action:Many images include direct calls to action like "Apply Now," "Book A Consultation," and "Partner with Skill Search," encouraging user engagement.
  • User-Centric:The content is tailored to address potential candidates and clients, highlighting benefits like ethical recruitment, proven screening processes, and tailored services.
Color:
  • Monochromatic and Minimalistic:The color scheme appears to be simple, often using a monochromatic palette with shades of blue or gray, which conveys professionalism and trust.
  • Contrast:High contrast between text and background ensures readability.
  • Accent Colors:Some images might use accent colors to highlight important information or calls to action, though the primary focus remains on simplicity.
Typography:
  • Sans-Serif Fonts:The use of clean, sans-serif fonts suggests modernity and approachability.
  • Hierarchy:Typography is used to create a clear hierarchy, with larger, bold fonts for headings and smaller fonts for body text.
  • Readability: The text is straightforward and easy to read, aligning with the professional tone of the brand.
Overall Impression:
  • The design is professional, clean, and focused on delivering a clear message. The use of consistent branding elements, simple color schemes, and readable typography ensures that the information is easily digestible and visually appealing. This approach aligns well with the target audience of job seekers and employers looking for reliable recruitment services.
