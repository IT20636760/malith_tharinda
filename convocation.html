Tools and Techniques

Equipment:
  • Canon EOS RP:I use the Canon EOS RP for its full-frame sensor and excellent low-light performance, which allows me to capture high-quality images with great detail and clarity.
  • Sigma 85mm Lens: The Sigma 85mm lens is perfect for portrait photography due to its sharpness and beautiful bokeh effect, which helps in isolating the subject from the background.
Lighting:
  • Natural Light: I prefer using natural light as it provides a soft and flattering illumination that enhances the natural beauty of the subject. I often shoot during the golden hours (early morning or late afternoon) to take advantage of the warm and diffused light.
Editing:
  • Adobe Photoshop:My editing process involves using Adobe Photoshop to fine-tune the images. I focus on color correction, adjusting exposure, and enhancing details to ensure the final image is polished and professional.
  • Techniques:I employ various techniques such as dodging and burning to add depth, frequency separation for skin retouching, and color grading to achieve a consistent and appealing look across my portfolio.
Overall Impression:
  • Composition: I pay close attention to composition, using techniques like the rule of thirds, leading lines, and framing to create visually appealing images. Post-Processing: My post-processing workflow includes RAW file editing to retain maximum detail, followed by selective adjustments to bring out the best in each photograph.
