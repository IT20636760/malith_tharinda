Project information

  • Category: Social Media design
  • Client: Tribe Yala
  • Project date: 01 March, 2024
  • Project URL: www.tribeyala.com

Social Media design for Instagram & Facebook

Design Thinking:
  • Consistency:The brand maintains a consistent theme of adventure, luxury, and eco-consciousness across all visuals. This reinforces the identity of Tribe Yala as a premium wildlife adventure company. Example: All designs feature earthy tones, wildlife imagery, and modern typography.
  • Clarity:The focus is on clear communication of Yala National Park’s unique offerings, such as wildlife safaris, birdwatching, and eco-friendly travel. Example: Informative posts like "Yala is home to 215 bird species" are easy to understand and visually engaging.
  • Call to Action:Many designs include direct calls to action like "Book Your Adventure," "Explore Yala’s Beauty," and "Join Us for a Safari," encouraging user engagement. Example: A promotional post with the text "20% Off Now – Book Your Safari Today!"
  • User-Centric:The content is tailored to address nature enthusiasts, adventure travelers, and eco-conscious individuals. It highlights benefits like luxury experiences, wildlife conservation, and sustainable travel. Example: Posts like "Pack for a Purpose – Eco-Friendly Travel Tips" resonate with eco-conscious travelers.
Color:
  • Earthy and Natural Palette:The color scheme is inspired by nature, using shades of green, brown, and gold to reflect Yala’s landscapes and wildlife. Example: Deep greens for the jungle, golden yellows for sunlight, and earthy browns for the terrain.
  • Contrast:High contrast between text and background ensures readability, especially in outdoor-themed designs. Example: White or light-colored text on dark green or brown backgrounds.
  • Accent Colors:Accent colors like orange or red are used sparingly to highlight important information or calls to action. Example: A bright orange "Book Now" button on a safari-themed post.
Typography:
  • Sans-Serif Fonts:Clean, modern sans-serif fonts are used to convey a sense of adventure and approachability. Example: Fonts like "Montserrat" or "Open Sans" for headings and body text.
  • Hierarchy:Typography is used to create a clear hierarchy, with larger, bold fonts for headings and smaller fonts for details. Example: "Discover Yala’s Wildlife" in a large, bold font, followed by smaller text describing the experience.
  • Readability: The text is straightforward and easy to read, aligning with the adventurous yet professional tone of the brand. Example: Short, impactful phrases like "Explore. Connect. Embrace the Wild."
Overall Impression:
  • The design is professional, clean, and focused on delivering a clear message. The use of consistent branding elements, simple color schemes, and readable typography ensures that the information is easily digestible and visually appealing. This approach aligns well with the target audience of job seekers and employers looking for reliable recruitment services.
